## How This Works

This is a living document repository. The Cookbook and supporting materials will evolve as:
- Elise provides missing data and reflections
- Stakeholder interviews are conducted (Section 2: Kitchen Notes)
- The next director begins operations and learns what actually works vs. what needs adjustment
- Replication attempts in other cities generate new learning

**Version Control:** Every change is tracked. You can see who changed what, when, and why. This helps the next director understand how decisions were made.
