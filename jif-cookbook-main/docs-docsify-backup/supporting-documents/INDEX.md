# Supporting Materials Index

This index lists all supporting documentation and replication assets for the Jerusalem International Fellows Cookbook website.

## Supporting Documents

| Title | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **2022 On Track Consulting Evaluation Report** | Independent assessment of the 2022 pilot residency cohort. Analyzes operational successes, participant feedback, and structural recommendations. |
| **Financial Operations Playbook** | Details organizational staffing cost structure, itemized fellow expenditures, and salon event economics. Provides baseline financial models to scale the residency across different city environments. |
| **2014-2018 Bellagio Strategic Plan** | Captures the five-year strategic plan from the Rockefeller Foundation Bellagio Center. Includes early infrastructure assumptions, expansion targets, and revenue projections. |
| **MailChimp Voice Synthesis Archive** | Compiles public newsletter campaigns sent to stakeholders between 2022 and 2025. Preserves the authentic institutional narrative, fellow milestones, and post-conflict updates. |
| **Press Archive Materials** | Aggregates news features, reviews, and profiles from publications like the Times of Israel and the Jerusalem Post. Documents the public reception and cultural diplomacy impact of the fellowship. |
