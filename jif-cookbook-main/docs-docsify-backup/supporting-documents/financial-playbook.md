# Jerusalem International Fellows: Financial Operations Playbook

## Staffing Cost Architecture and Role Framework

Sustainable program delivery requires five distinct staffing components. The organization must fund each role independently to prevent administrative bottlenecks:

1. **Executive Director (1.0 FTE):** Directs long-term strategy, institutional fundraising, board governance, and high-level donor cultivation.
   - Annual Salary Benchmarks: Kyiv: $30,000 | Krakow: $42,000 | Chicago: $65,000

2. **Program Director (1.0 FTE):** Manages local partner relationships, daily fellow logistics, housing infrastructure, and on-ground problem-solving. Requires a hiring lead time of twelve to sixteen weeks prior to cohort arrival.
   - Annual Salary Benchmarks: Kyiv: $28,000 | Krakow: $38,000 | Chicago: $58,000

3. **Communications and Salon Manager (1.0 FTE):** Coordinates weekly guest curation, multilingual invitation workflows, translation logistics, and social media tracking.
   - Annual Salary Benchmarks: Kyiv: $18,000 | Krakow: $26,000 | Chicago: $40,000

4. **Finance Administrator (0.5 FTE):** Manages payroll, international currency transfers, local tax compliance, and grant acquittal tracking.
   - Annual Salary Benchmarks: Kyiv: $6,000 | Krakow: $8,000 | Chicago: $12,500

5. **Host Organization Coordinators (Part-Time Contracted):** Provides daily on-site translation and project support within each host institution.
   - Allocation Per Unit: Kyiv: $5,000 | Krakow: $6,500 | Chicago: $10,000

## Per-Fellow Direct Expenditure Profiles (10-Week Cycle)

Direct costs remain fixed per fellow and scale linearly with the cohort size:

**Kyiv Baseline Model ($6,710 Total Per Fellow):** Airfare: $900 | Housing (84 nights): $1,512 | Individual Meals: $1,008 | Salon Catering: $1,440 | Transport: $600 | Project Materials: $250 | Insurance Allocation: $1,000

**Krakow Baseline Model ($9,912 Total Per Fellow):** Airfare: $900 | Housing (84 nights): $2,940 | Individual Meals: $1,512 | Salon Catering: $2,160 | Transport: $600 | Project Materials: $300 | Insurance Allocation: $1,500

**Chicago Baseline Model ($16,032 Total Per Fellow):** Airfare: $800 | Housing (84 nights): $40,320 | Individual Meals: $14,112 | Salon Catering: $5,040 | Transport: $2,400 | Project Materials: $400 | Insurance Allocation: $12,000

## Operational Cash Outflow Timeline

- **Hiring and Infrastructure Phase (Weeks -16 to -8):** Deploys 45% of the total operating budget to secure core staff contracts, upfront insurance policies, and housing security deposits.

- **Ramp-Up and Invitation Phase (Weeks -8 to -1):** Funds communications management, multilingual website setup, and preliminary salon curation frameworks.

- **Cohort Arrival Protocol (Week 0):** Generates the sharpest cash burn through immediate flight reimbursements, upfront rent blocks, and disorientation guide outlays.

- **Active Residency Phase (Weeks 1-9):** Absorbs weekly outlays for staff salaries, culinary salon execution, materials stipends, and coordinator disbursements.

- **Closeout and Archive Phase (Week 10+):** Funds final independent evaluations, digital file archiving, and sponsor reporting packages.
