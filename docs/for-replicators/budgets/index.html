Financial Management Principles

  1. The Deductibility Constraint: Several municipal tax frameworks exclude food and beverages from standard deduction lists. Replicators must budget catering outlays separately or issue higher daily meal stipends directly to fellows to bypass receipt-tracking friction.

  2. Fixed Cost Efficiency: Fixed administration driving the core engine remains constant. Expanding a cohort from four to six fellows adds minimal operational cost but increases fellow footprint efficiency by 14%.

  3. Diversified Pyramid Revenue: Replicators must secure a diversified funding base where no single foundation grant exceeds 50% of the annual ledger.
