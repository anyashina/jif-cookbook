# Replication Manual: Staffing Models and Delegation Guidelines

## Core Organizational Architecture

The program functions through a clear division of labor between strategic governance and local, on-ground execution. Replicators must fund five essential core components:

```
[Executive Director] -- Strategy, Board Governance, Major Donors
    |
[Program Director] -- Local Operations, Host Logistics, Fellow Support
    |
[Communications Manager] -- Salon Curation, Multilingual PR, Translations
    |
[Finance Administrator] (0.5 FTE) -- Budget Tracking, Invoicing, Tax Compliance
    |
[Host Organization Coordinators] (Part-Time) -- On-Site Fellow Embedding
```

## Detailed Capability Requirements

**Executive Director (1.0 FTE):** Leads multi-year strategy, manages board alignment, cultivates anchor foundations, and secures institutional funding. This role operates internationally and handles final budgetary approvals.

**Program Director (1.0 FTE):** Owns on-ground operations, tracks fellow safety, manages neighborhood housing, and resolves partner conflicts. Requires complete bilingual literacy and verified operational networks within the host city.

**Communications and Salon Manager (1.0 FTE):** Curates local invitation lists, coordinates calendar logistics, translates conceptual event blocks, and documents operations. This role requires full-time capacity due to managing three weekly salon events.

**Finance Administrator (0.5 FTE):** Manages multi-currency transactions, processes vendor invoices, tracks line-item variances, and enforces local non-profit compliance. This position must remain separate from operational leadership to protect budget transparency.

**Host Organization Coordinators (0.5 FTE Per Anchor):** Embedded directly within each host space to guide fellows through daily neighborhood logistics, maintain material supplies, and facilitate immediate team translation.

## Recruitment and Onboarding Lifecycles

- **16 Weeks Pre-Arrival:** Lock in the Executive Director contract and finalize the annual operating ledger.
- **12 Weeks Pre-Arrival:** Complete the Program Director hire and initiate the Disorientation Week itinerary mapping.
- **8 Weeks Pre-Arrival:** Onboard the Communications Manager and contract the Finance firm.
- **4 Weeks Pre-Arrival:** Finalize part-time contracts for all Host Organization Coordinators.
- **2 Weeks Pre-Arrival:** Conduct full-team alignment rehearsals, walking through safety escalation trees and salon setup workflows.
